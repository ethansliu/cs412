About Steve Jobs

Steve Paul Jobs is an American Inventor and Entrepreneur who is most well known for cofounding the company Apple Inc. He later became the CEO of Apple, and inspired millions with his forward thinking and design ethos. Under his leadership at Apple, iconic and life-changing products such as the iPhone, iPod, iPad, and Mac Computers were created.
